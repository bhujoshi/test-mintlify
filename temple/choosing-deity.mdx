Divine Presence is home to multiple deity shrines, each one a distinct sacred space with its own architectural style, color palette, associated offerings, and devotional mantras. Choosing your deity is not a one-time decision — you can visit any shrine during a session and switch your home shrine whenever you like. Start with the deity whose blessings you seek today, and let your practice grow from there.

## Available Deity Shrines

Each shrine is lovingly designed to reflect the iconography, colors, and sacred associations of its deity. Free tier devotees have access to the Ganesha, Lakshmi, and Shiva shrines. Upgrade to premium to unlock the full pantheon.

## How to Switch Deity Shrines

You can move between shrines at any point during your session using the Shrine Selector. Your current offerings and session progress are preserved when you switch.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Shrine Selector">
    On desktop, press `E` or click the shrine icon (a small temple silhouette) in the top toolbar. On mobile, tap the shrine icon in the bottom toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the Available Shrines">
    The Shrine Selector panel slides in from the right, displaying all deity shrines as illustrated cards. Free-tier shrines appear in full color; premium-locked shrines appear dimmed with a lock icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Deity">
    Click or tap the card for the deity you wish to visit. A brief preview panel appears on the right, showing the shrine's visual theme, associated offerings, and a short description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Travel">
    Click or tap **Visit Shrine** to confirm. The screen fades and you are transported to the entrance of that deity's sanctum, ready to begin your offering.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as Home Shrine (Optional)">
    Inside the shrine, tap the **☆ Set as Home** button in the top toolbar to make this deity your default starting point for future sessions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I worship multiple deities in a single session?">
    Yes. You can visit as many shrines as you like within a single session — simply open the Shrine Selector and travel between them at any time. Each shrine keeps its own offering tray, so offerings you make at Ganesha's shrine are separate from those at Lakshmi's shrine. There is no limit to how many shrines you visit or how many offerings you complete in one session.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I unlock premium deities?">
    Premium deities — Krishna, Durga, and Saraswati — are available with a Divine Presence Premium subscription. To upgrade, navigate to **Settings → Subscription** and select a monthly or annual plan. Once your subscription is active, all premium shrines unlock immediately without needing to restart the app. You can also gift a premium subscription to another devotee from the same settings page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch my home shrine?">
    Absolutely. Your home shrine can be changed as often as you like. Visit any shrine, then tap the **☆ Set as Home** button in the top toolbar while you are inside that sanctum. The change takes effect immediately — the next time you open Divine Presence, you will arrive at your newly chosen home shrine. Note that switching your home shrine does not erase your offering history at your previous shrine.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
