Moving through the Divine Presence temple is designed to feel natural and unhurried, whether you are on a desktop browser or a mobile device. You can walk freely through every zone, turn to gaze at the carved pillars and lamp niches, and reach out to interact with sacred objects along the way. This page covers all the controls you need to explore the temple and begin your offering.

## Controls by Device

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop">
    Use your keyboard to walk and your mouse to look around — the same controls used in most 3D exploration experiences.

    | Action | Control |
    |---|---|
    | Walk forward / backward | `W` / `S` or `↑` / `↓` |
    | Strafe left / right | `A` / `D` or `←` / `→` |
    | Look around | Click and drag the mouse |
    | Interact with an object | Left-click on it |
    | Zoom in / out | Scroll the mouse wheel |
    | Open the Shrine Selector | Press `E` or click the shrine icon in the toolbar |
    | Pause / open menu | `Escape` |

    When you hover over an interactive object — such as an offering item, a bell, or a lamp — a soft highlight appears around it and a tooltip shows the available action. Click to trigger it.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile">
    The touch interface is built for one-handed and two-handed use, keeping the most common actions within easy reach.

    | Action | Control |
    |---|---|
    | Look around | Swipe anywhere on the screen |
    | Move to a location | Tap the floor where you want to walk |
    | Interact with an object | Tap directly on it |
    | Zoom in / out | Pinch with two fingers |
    | Open the Shrine Selector | Tap the shrine icon in the bottom toolbar |
    | Pause / open menu | Tap the hamburger menu (top left) |

    On smaller screens, interactive objects display a tap-target ring to make them easier to select accurately. If you miss an object, try tapping the center of the glowing ring rather than the edges of the 3D model.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Camera Modes

Divine Presence offers three camera modes to suit different moments in your visit. The mode changes automatically in certain zones, but you can also switch manually at any time from the camera icon in the top toolbar.

**Free Roam** is the default mode. The camera follows you from a third-person perspective as you walk anywhere in the temple.

**Deity Focus** locks the camera to face the deity idol directly, gently rotating to keep the idol centered as you move closer or farther. This mode is ideal for meditation and mantra recitation.

**Offering Mode** zooms the camera into a close-up view of the offering tray in front of the idol, giving you a clear, intimate view of each item as you present it.

<Tip>
  Switch to **Deity Focus** mode before you begin your offering. Keeping the deity's gaze at the center of your screen deepens the sense of presence and helps you stay focused throughout the ritual, especially if you are reciting mantras at the same time.
</Tip>

## Entering Offering Mode

Offering Mode activates automatically when you step onto the Offering Podium, but you can also enter it manually from anywhere in the sanctum.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Approach the Offering Podium">
    Walk toward the brass tray placed in front of the deity idol. As you get close, a soft golden glow outlines the podium to indicate it is interactive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interact with the Tray">
    On desktop, left-click the tray. On mobile, tap it. The camera smoothly transitions into the close-up Offering Mode view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Offerings">
    Your offering inventory appears at the bottom of the screen. Tap or click each item — flowers, water, incense, or a lamp — to present it to the deity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Offering">
    Once you have offered all your items, tap or click **Complete Offering**. The deity idol briefly radiates light, and a blessing message appears on screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Exit Offering Mode">
    Press `Escape` on desktop or tap the **×** button on mobile to return to Free Roam and continue exploring the temple.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Mini-Map

A small mini-map sits in the **top-right corner** of the screen at all times. It shows a top-down schematic of the temple with your current position marked as a glowing dot. Each zone — Entrance Hall, Main Sanctum, Offering Podium, and Prayer Corner — is color-coded and labeled.

Tap or click the mini-map to expand it to full screen for easier orientation. Tap or click again, or press `M` on desktop, to collapse it.
