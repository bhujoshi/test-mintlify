Offering flowers — known as **pushpanjali** — is the most fundamental act of worship in Hindu tradition. In Divine Presence, you reach into the 3D offering tray, select your chosen blooms, and present them at your deity's feet. A beautiful petal-fall animation fills the shrine as each flower drifts gently downward, making every offering feel alive and sacred.

## How to Offer Flowers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Offerings Panel">
    Tap the **lotus icon** in the bottom navigation bar to open the Offerings Panel. The panel slides up from the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Flowers Tab">
    Inside the Offerings Panel, tap the **Flowers** tab. You will see all flower types available for your currently selected deity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Flower">
    Browse the flower tray and tap the bloom you wish to offer. Each flower card shows the flower name and its traditional significance. Greyed-out flowers are not traditionally associated with the active deity.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Tap "Offer"'>
    Tap the **Offer** button beneath your chosen flower. Your avatar's hands rise toward the shrine in a reverent gesture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch the 3D Animation">
    Petals stream from your cupped hands and cascade to the deity's feet in a flowing 3D animation. The shrine floor gradually fills with colour as your offerings accumulate throughout the session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Flowers by Deity

Each deity in Divine Presence has flowers that hold special traditional significance. Offering these preferred blooms displays an enhanced golden shimmer on the idol.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="🌼 Marigold" icon="sun">
    Bright and auspicious, marigolds are beloved by **Ganesha** and **Durga**. Their vivid orange petals symbolise prosperity and the removal of obstacles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🌹 Red Rose" icon="heart">
    The red rose carries devotion and passion. It is a cherished offering for **Ganesha**, evoking deep personal love for the remover of obstacles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🪷 Lotus" icon="droplet">
    The lotus rises from muddy water in perfect purity — a symbol of divine grace. It is the premier offering for **Lakshmi** and **Saraswati**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🤍 White Jasmine" icon="sparkles">
    Delicate and fragrant, white jasmine conveys innocence and devotion. **Lakshmi** is particularly pleased by this gentle bloom.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🍃 Bilva Leaves" icon="leaf">
    The three-lobed bilva leaf represents the three eyes of **Shiva** and the Trimurti. It is considered the most sacred offering for Shiva devotees.
  </Card>
  <Card title="💙 Blue Lotus" icon="star">
    Rare and otherworldly, the blue lotus is sacred to **Shiva**. Its deep colour reflects his transcendence beyond the material world.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🌿 Tulsi" icon="leaf">
    Tulsi (holy basil) is among the most sacred plants in Vaishnavism. **Krishna** is known to accept any offering when tulsi leaves are included.
  </Card>
  <Card title="💛 Yellow Marigold" icon="sun">
    Yellow marigolds radiate joy and auspiciousness, making them a favourite of **Krishna** during festive worship.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🌺 Red Hibiscus" icon="flame">
    Vibrant red hibiscus is the power flower of **Durga**, representing her fierce, protective energy and victory over darkness.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🌸 White Chrysanthemum" icon="snowflake">
    Pure white chrysanthemums evoke learning, clarity, and creative wisdom — the ideal offering for **Saraswati**, goddess of knowledge and the arts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Tips for Your Flower Offerings

<Tip>
  **Pushpanjali Premium Gesture** — Unlock the **Pushpanjali** gesture in a Premium subscription to offer flowers with both hands cupped together in the traditional anjali mudra. The enhanced animation shows petals floating upward before descending, creating a truly immersive moment of devotion.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Flower petals offered during your session remain visible on the shrine floor until you close the temple. Each new offering adds to the growing carpet of blooms, giving you a beautiful visual record of your worship.
</Note>

## Devotion Streak

Every flower offering you complete counts toward your **daily devotion streak**. Return each day to maintain your streak — the app highlights your streak count on the Home screen. Consecutive-day streaks unlock special seasonal flower varieties and shrine decorations.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why is my favourite flower greyed out?">
    Flowers that are not traditionally associated with the currently active deity are shown as greyed out. Switch to the deity for whom that flower is preferred, or unlock the **Free Offering** mode in Settings to offer any flower to any deity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I offer multiple flowers in one session?">
    Yes — you can offer as many flowers as you like in a single session. Each offering triggers its own petal animation and adds individually to your devotion streak counter for the day.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I unlock rare flowers?">
    Rare flowers such as the blue lotus and white chrysanthemum are unlocked by reaching devotion milestones or through seasonal in-app events. Check the **Rewards** section on the Home screen for current unlock conditions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
