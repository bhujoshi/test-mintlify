## Begin Your Journey

Follow these three steps to go from first visit to your first completed puja.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Register with your email address or sign in with a social account. Your profile stores your deity preferences, worship history, and personal altar settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Deity">
    Browse the full pantheon and select the deity at whose feet you wish to begin your practice. You can add more deities to your home altar at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin Your First Offering">
    Enter the temple, pick up a flower from the offering tray, and place it at the deity's feet. Your worship streak begins with this single, heartfelt act.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  🙏 **You are welcome here.** Every tradition, every background, every level of experience is honoured within these virtual walls. May your time in the temple bring you peace, clarity, and a deeper sense of the sacred.
</Note>
