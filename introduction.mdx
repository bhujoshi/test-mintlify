Divine Presence is a real-time 3D worship platform that lets you perform puja — traditional devotional worship — from any device with a web browser. Inside the virtual temple you can light incense, offer fresh flowers, pour sacred water, and wave aarti lamps before a richly rendered deity shrine, all accompanied by devotional music and guided mantras. The experience is designed to honour the sincerity of genuine practice: every offering you make is acknowledged with ambient light, sound, and visual feedback that mirrors the atmosphere of a living temple.

## Compatibility

Divine Presence runs entirely in your web browser — there is nothing to download or install. It works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge) on desktop, laptop, and mobile devices. For the richest visual experience, a device released in the last four years with hardware-accelerated graphics is recommended, but the platform is designed to scale gracefully on older hardware as well.

<Tip>
  For the most immersive experience, put on a pair of headphones before you enter the temple. The spatial audio — bells on the left, flowing water on the right, chanting from the shrine ahead — places you firmly inside the sacred space in a way that speakers alone cannot replicate.
</Tip>
