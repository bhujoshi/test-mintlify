This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a blank screen to your first completed puja. The entire process takes about five minutes, and you will leave with a worship streak already underway. All you need is a web browser and a few moments of quiet intention.

<Note>
  Divine Presence requires **no downloads, plugins, or app installations**. The full 3D temple experience runs directly inside any modern web browser on desktop or mobile.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Open [app.divinepresence.com](https://app.divinepresence.com) and click **Get Started — It's Free**.

    Choose how you would like to register:
    - **Email** — Enter your email address and choose a password, then verify your inbox.
    - **Google or Apple** — Click the corresponding button and authorise access in the pop-up window.

    Once you are signed in, you land on the **Welcome Screen**, which guides you through a short setup flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Deity">
    The deity selection screen shows the full pantheon available on your plan. Scroll through the gallery and click any deity to read a short description of their significance and the traditional offerings associated with them.

    Click **Set as My Home Deity** to confirm your choice. You can change this later from your altar settings, and free-tier accounts can add up to three deities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the Temple">
    Click **Enter Temple** on your dashboard. The 3D environment loads in your browser — this usually takes under ten seconds on a standard connection.

    Once inside, you will see the main hall ahead of you and your chosen deity's shrine directly in front. The offering tray sits at the base of the shrine, filled with fresh flowers, water vessels, incense sticks, and an aarti lamp.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Perform Your First Flower Offering">
    To make your first offering:

    1. Click or tap a **flower** on the offering tray to pick it up.
    2. Move your cursor (or drag on mobile) toward the deity's feet until the placement highlight glows gold.
    3. Click or tap again to **place the flower** as your offering.

    A soft bell tone and a warm glow confirm that your offering has been received. Your daily worship streak now shows **Day 1**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
