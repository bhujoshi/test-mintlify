Your temple in Divine Presence is not limited to a single deity. You can open dedicated shrines for multiple gods and goddesses, moving between them during a single worship session. One deity is always designated as your **home deity** — the shrine that greets you the moment you enter. The others remain accessible from the Shrine Switcher at any time, so your complete devotional practice lives in one place.

## Adding a New Deity Shrine

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Temple Management">
    From inside the temple, tap the **Manage Shrines** button in the bottom navigation bar. This opens the Temple Management panel listing your current shrines and available deities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Available Deities">
    Scroll through the deity gallery. Free-tier deities are shown with an open icon; premium deities display a crown badge. Tap any deity card to see their 3D idol preview, associated color theme, traditional offerings, and linked mantras.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the Deity to Your Temple">
    Tap **Add Shrine** on the deity card. The shrine is immediately created in your temple and added to the Shrine Switcher in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as Home Deity (Optional)">
    If you want this deity to be the first shrine you see when you enter the temple, open **Shrine Settings** (the gear icon on the shrine card) and tap **Set as Home Deity**. Your previous home deity remains in your temple — it just moves to second position in the switcher.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange the Shrine">
    Enter the new shrine and use the **Altar Editor** to personalize the platform, cloth, and offerings for this specific deity. Each shrine maintains its own independent layout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing a Deity Shrine

To remove a shrine, open **Manage Shrines**, tap the gear icon on the deity card, and select **Remove Shrine**. A confirmation prompt will appear because removal clears all altar arrangements saved for that shrine. Your home deity cannot be removed unless you first designate a different home deity.

<Note>
  Premium subscribers can maintain up to **6 active deity shrines** simultaneously. Free accounts support up to 2 active shrines. You can always swap which deities are active in Temple Management without losing their saved altar arrangements.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Use the **Shrine Switcher** in the bottom navigation bar to jump between your shrines without returning to the main temple hall. A long press on any deity icon in the switcher lets you reorder shrines or mark a new home deity on the spot.
</Tip>
