Your Divine Presence account is the foundation of your personal practice — it stores your altar layout, worship history, deity preferences, and streak data so that every session picks up exactly where you left off. This guide walks you through creating your account and configuring the settings that shape your daily experience.

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Registration Page">
    Navigate to [app.divinepresence.com](https://app.divinepresence.com) and click **Get Started — It's Free**. No credit card is required to create a free account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Sign-In Method">
    Select one of the following options:

    - **Email & Password** — Enter your email address and a secure password, then open the verification email and click the confirmation link.
    - **Sign in with Google** — Click the Google button and select your Google account in the pop-up. No separate password is needed.
    - **Sign in with Apple** — Click the Apple button and authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.

    All three methods create the same full account with identical access to the free tier features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Display Name">
    Enter the name that will appear on your profile, worship logs, and any community features. This can be your real name, a spiritual name, or any name that feels meaningful to you. You can update it at any time from **Settings → Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Home Deity Preference">
    Browse the deity gallery and select the deity whose shrine will greet you each time you enter the temple. Click a deity's card to read about their significance, associated offerings, and traditional days of worship, then click **Set as Home Deity** to confirm.

    Free-tier accounts can add **up to three deities** to their personal altar. You can swap your home deity as often as you like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Daily Worship Reminders">
    On the **Notifications** setup screen, toggle on **Daily Worship Reminder**. Choose your preferred time from the time-picker — this is the time you will receive a gentle push notification (or email, if you prefer) reminding you to complete today's offering and maintain your streak.

    You can change your reminder time or disable notifications at any time from **Settings → Notifications**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Plan and Finish Setup">
    The final setup screen summarises what is included in your free account and what is available on the premium plan. Read through the comparison, then click **Start My Practice** to enter the temple for the first time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Choose a reminder time that consistently falls within a quiet part of your day — early morning before the household wakes up, or the few minutes after your evening meal tend to work best for most devotees. A predictable time helps worship become a natural rhythm rather than a task to remember.
</Tip>

## Free Tier vs. Premium

Divine Presence is free to use, and the free tier is designed to support a genuine daily practice. Premium unlocks the full depth of the platform for devotees who want more.

| Feature | Free | Premium |
|---|---|---|
| Deities available | 3 | All deities in the full pantheon |
| Offering types | Flowers & water | All offerings (incense, aarti lamps, food, and more) |
| Personal altar | Single layout | Unlimited saved altar configurations |
| Devotional soundscapes | Standard library | Extended library with rare regional compositions |
| Daily streak tracking | ✓ | ✓ |
| Mantra guidance | ✓ | ✓ |
| Mobile access | ✓ | ✓ |

<Info>
  To upgrade to Premium at any time, go to **Settings → Subscription** and choose a monthly or annual plan. Annual subscribers receive two months free compared to monthly billing.
</Info>

## Managing Your Account Settings

After setup is complete, you can return to any of these preferences from your account dashboard:

- **Profile** — Update your display name, profile photo, and spiritual biography.
- **Altar** — Add or remove deities, rearrange your altar layout, and save multiple configurations (Premium).
- **Notifications** — Change your daily reminder time, choose between push and email notifications, or disable reminders entirely.
- **Subscription** — Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan, and view your billing history.
- **Data & Privacy** — Download your worship history or permanently delete your account.

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All worship history, streak data, altar configurations, and saved preferences will be removed immediately. Export your worship history from **Settings → Data & Privacy** before proceeding if you wish to keep a record.
</Warning>
