## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Divine Presence?">
    Divine Presence is a 3D immersive worship experience that lets you offer flowers, water, incense, and lamps to deities inside a beautifully rendered virtual temple. You can perform traditional rituals, maintain a personal altar, and observe sacred festivals — all from your browser or mobile device, at any time of day.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which deities are supported?">
    The free tier gives you access to three deities: **Ganesha**, **Lakshmi**, and **Shiva**. A premium subscription unlocks the full pantheon of **12 deities**, including additional forms and festival-specific avatars that appear during Festive Mode events.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does it work on mobile devices?">
    Yes. Divine Presence runs on modern Android and iOS browsers that support WebGL 2.0. For the best mobile experience, use Chrome for Android or Safari on iOS 14 and above. Some advanced features — such as Pushpanjali gesture recognition and Mantra Mode — are available on mobile but perform best on mid-range or higher devices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is an account required to use Divine Presence?">
    You can explore the temple and observe worship without signing in. However, an account is required to save your altar layout, record your worship history, schedule offerings, and access any subscription features. Creating an account is free and takes less than a minute.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What browsers are supported?">
    Divine Presence supports the following browsers and minimum versions:

    | Browser | Minimum Version |
    |---------|----------------|
    | Chrome  | 90+            |
    | Firefox | 88+            |
    | Safari  | 14+            |
    | Edge    | 90+            |

    For the smoothest 3D rendering and WebGL performance, **Chrome 90+** is recommended. Make sure your browser is up to date before your first visit.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Offerings & Worship

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which offerings can I make?">
    All users — including those on the free tier — can make the following basic offerings:

    - **Pushpa** (flowers)
    - **Jal** (water)
    - **Dhoop** (incense)
    - **Deepam** (lamp / Aarti flame)

    Premium subscribers additionally unlock **Panchamrit** (the five-nectar abhishek ritual), fruit offerings, cloth offerings, and specialty items tied to each deity's tradition.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I perform abhishek (water offering)?">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Offering Mode">
        Click or tap the **Offer** button in the bottom toolbar. The camera will automatically focus on the deity's idol.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select the Jal offering">
        Choose **Jal (Water)** from the offering tray that slides in from the left.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Pour the offering">
        Click and drag (or press and hold on mobile) over the idol to pour the water in an arc. The animation completes when the water stream finishes and the idol glows briefly.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive prasad confirmation">
        A prasad confirmation card appears at the top of the screen, logging the offering to your worship history.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Premium subscribers can perform **Panchamrit abhishek** — a ritual pour of milk, honey, sugar, ghee, and yoghurt — by selecting the Panchamrit option in the same offering tray.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the Pushpanjali gesture?">
    Pushpanjali is the traditional gesture of offering flowers with both hands cupped together. In Divine Presence, activating **Pushpanjali Mode** (premium) enables camera-based hand tracking so your physical cupped-hands gesture triggers the flower shower animation on screen. You can also perform Pushpanjali using the click-and-hold method without hand tracking if you prefer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I schedule offerings in advance?">
    Yes, with a free or premium account you can schedule a daily offering reminder. Go to **Settings > Notifications** and set a time for your daily worship reminder. Premium subscribers can additionally pre-schedule specific offering sequences — for example, a morning Aarti and an evening flower offering — which the platform performs automatically if you are logged in and the browser tab is open.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my offerings — are they recorded?">
    Every offering you make while signed in is written to your **Worship Journal**, accessible from your profile page. Each entry records the date, time, deity, offering type, and any mantra you recited. Your journal is private by default; you can choose to share individual entries or a monthly summary from the journal settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Subscription

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is included in the free tier?">
    The free tier gives every registered user access to:

    - **3 deities**: Ganesha, Lakshmi, and Shiva
    - **Basic offering types**: flowers, water, incense, and lamp
    - **15 altar decoration items** from the catalog
    - **1 active shrine** in your personal temple space
    - Full worship history and journal
    - Daily reminder notifications
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does a premium subscription unlock?">
    Upgrading to premium gives you the complete Divine Presence experience:

    - **All 12 deities**, including festival-specific deity forms
    - **All offering types**, including Panchamrit abhishek and specialty deity offerings
    - **Full altar catalog** with hundreds of decoration and accessory items
    - **6 active shrines** — build a full personal temple
    - **Pushpanjali gesture** — hand-tracked flower offering
    - **Japa Mala auto-count** — tracks your mantra repetitions automatically
    - **Mantra Mode** — guided mantra sessions with synchronized visual effects
    - **Festive Mode** — special decorations and rituals tied to the Hindu calendar
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade to premium?">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your account menu">
        Click your avatar in the top-right corner of the screen and select **Upgrade to Premium**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a billing cycle">
        Select either the **monthly** or **annual** plan. The annual plan offers a significant discount compared to monthly billing.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete payment">
        Enter your payment details on the secure checkout page. Divine Presence accepts all major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enjoy premium features">
        Your account upgrades instantly. Refresh the temple page to see all premium deities, offerings, and features unlocked.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription anytime?">
    Yes. You can cancel at any time from **Account Settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep full premium access until that date. After cancellation, your account reverts to the free tier and your altar is preserved, though items beyond the free-tier limits will be archived until you resubscribe.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my worship history saved?">
    Your worship history and Worship Journal are stored securely in the cloud and tied to your account. They persist across devices, browsers, and sessions. Even if you cancel a premium subscription, your full historical journal remains accessible. You can export your journal as a PDF or CSV from **Profile > Worship Journal > Export**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why is the 3D temple loading slowly?">
    Temple load times depend on your internet connection speed and device capability. Check that you have a stable connection, then try lowering the graphics quality in **Settings > Graphics** to **Medium** or **Low**. Disabling particle effects in the same menu can also significantly improve frame rate on older devices. Closing unused browser tabs frees up memory, and switching to Chrome often gives the best WebGL rendering performance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why can't I hear the devotional music?">
    Most browsers block audio from playing automatically until you interact with the page. Click or tap anywhere inside the temple to allow audio to begin. Also verify that your device is not on silent or Do Not Disturb mode. If audio still does not play, open **Settings > Audio** and confirm that **Music Volume** is above zero. Finally, check your browser's site permissions to ensure Divine Presence is allowed to play audio.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I reset my altar to default?">
    Go to **Settings > Altar > Reset to Default**. This removes all custom decorations and returns your shrine to the original layout. 
    
    <Warning>
      Resetting your altar is permanent and cannot be undone. Your worship history and journal entries are not affected, but any altar arrangement you have built will be lost.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My offering animation didn't play — what should I do?">
    First, confirm you are in **Offering Mode** — the camera should be zoomed in and focused on the deity before you select an offering. If the animation still does not appear, verify that WebGL is enabled in your browser settings. Refreshing the page and attempting the offering again resolves the issue in most cases. On mobile, make sure the Divine Presence browser tab is in the foreground and the screen has not timed out.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
